WHAT IS HIRO?



Hiro brings active investing to decentralized launchpads, ensuring long-term support and success for projects in the Hiro incubation schemes. The Hiro platform couples innovative entrepreneurship in DeFi with a top-of-the-line accelerator program that decentralizes active participation and advice through innovative consensus methods. Hiro introduces participation-based reward structures to web 3.0, which promises forward-looking development and lasting value. As a decentralized autonomous organization, it is as strong as the sum of its parts and is designed to attract and rewards only the strongest parts.
